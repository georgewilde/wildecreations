About

George Wilde

Overview

I am a senior front end developer with over 14 years of commercial experience building service-oriented web applications.

Using JavaScript, Angular, TypeScript, HTML5 and CSS3 I have led the development of many high-performance and responsive websites across various industries.

I have extensive experience in Angular 2-5, AngularJS, Redux (NgRx) and many responsive frameworks such as Bootstrap, Foundation, PureCSS.

I am passionate about delivering high-quality solutions fast and am a huge advocate for Continuous Delivery. I am also comfortable working in a Scrum or Kanban environment.
